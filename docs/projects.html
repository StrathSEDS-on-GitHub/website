Our Projects

The StrathSEDS Project team works on innovative projects and competitions to show off our skills and knowledge.

The Mach-22 Launch Competition

The RF Magic mission was a category winning cansat and rocket combination developed in 2021/22. The project involved 3 radios, 2 cansats and a rocket, with an intended apogee of 1km. On launch day, we reached 1009.94m and successfully deployed all systems.

Mach-21

Small scale simulation of STRATHcube, a student let cubesat project at Strathclyde

UKube-1

StrathSEDS was part of the UK's first ever CubeSat launch.

National Rocketry Competition 2014

StrathSEDS' team Just Testing won the National Rocketry Competition held by UKSEDS in 2014.
