Do you like space?

Because we do.

Strathclyde's space society since 2011.

StrathSEDS runs talks, events and workshops aimed at giving students a link to the space industry. During the semester we hold meetings on Tuesdays and Thursdays at 5pm. On Tuesdays we hold one-off sessions designed to give students an environment to grow their interest in space and gain skills necassary to work in the industry. Thursdays are dedicated to working on projects such as Strathosphere I.

Mach-22 Launch Competition

For this year's launch competition, operated by UKLSL, Discover Space, and UKSEDS, we are designing a high-powered rocket - whose purpose is to deliver a payload to an altitude of 1km - and two CanSats, drinks-can sized satellites that can relay a signal from between two ground stations.

How it works

The Launch Vehicle

Strathosphere I is our high-powered rocket to carry our ChatSats - Motor details (600Ns Impulse, 300N max thrust) - Dimensions (1050mm long, 78mm diameter) - Max speed (mach 0.5) - Acceleration (10g)
